Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

What types of projects do you handle?

We handle a wide range of construction projects including commercial, residential, industrial, and renovation projects of various sizes and complexities.

How do I get a quote for my project?

You can request a quote by filling out our contact form, calling our office, or sending us an email with details about your project. We'll arrange a consultation to discuss your needs.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, SBC Construction is fully licensed and insured. We maintain all necessary certifications and insurance coverage to protect our clients and projects.

How long does a typical construction project take?

Project timelines vary depending on size, complexity, and specific requirements. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline estimate for your specific project.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. The specific warranty terms depend on the type of project and will be outlined in your contract.

Do you handle permits and approvals?

Yes, we manage all necessary permits, inspections, and regulatory approvals as part of our comprehensive service to ensure your project complies with all local regulations.
